What Is OneSure?

OneSure is a corporate insurance platform that can be signed up online.
You can use for free the platform in which simple estimation, quick sign-up, and collective management are available, greatly improving operation efficiency.

Do You Have the Following Issues Related to Corporate Insurance?

Difficult to Estimate

  • Unable to get an estimation promptly
  • Need to answer a lot of questions in order to get a rough estimation
  • Takes a long time to check with other departments in order to fill in the form

Takes a Long Time to Sign Up

  • Takes a long time to fill out and send the paper-based form
  • Takes time to communicate with executives owing to a lot of documents that require the seal of the approver

Complicated Paper Management

  • Different insurance companies according to the type of insurance, and takes time just to confirm the coverage conditions and contact information
  • Owing to the lack of proper management, difficult to take over when changing the person in charge

Three Features of OneSure

Simple Estimation

Up to five items need to be filled out depending on the type of insurance you want. Your insurance premium will be estimated in about one minute.

Quick Sign-up

Once you are satisfied with the insurance plan, you can sign the contract online with no seal required. You can obtain insurance in about five minutes.

Collective Management

Paperless enables collective management of insurance policies and contact information in case of an accident. The data is integrated into My Page, so you do not have to worry about paperwork.
